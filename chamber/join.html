Membership Levels

NP Membership

Designed for nonprofit organizations with no fee and access to member events, community listings, and volunteer networks.

Bronze Membership

Perfect for new businesses. Includes basic promotion, event invites, and access to training sessions.

Silver Membership

For growing organizations: includes advertising opportunities, event discounts, and member spotlight features.

Gold Membership

Premium support with VIP event access, expanded advertising, training credits, and leadership opportunities.

NP Membership Benefits

NP Membership is ideal for nonprofits looking for community presence without a membership fee.

  • Access to member networking events
  • Community directory listing
  • Volunteer and sponsorship matchmaking
  • Invitations to nonprofit roundtables

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze members gain foundational exposure and access to practical Chamber services.

  • Promoted business listing
  • Training workshop access
  • Invitations to networking mixers
  • Basic event discounts

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver-level members receive stronger visibility and preferred access to member programs.

  • Featured advertising opportunities
  • Spotlight positions on the home page
  • Premium event discounts
  • Additional training resources

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold members enjoy premium recognition and expanded Chamber support.

  • VIP event access and sponsorship opportunities
  • Enhanced advertising and spotlight positions
  • Custom training programs
  • Priority introductions and executive networking
